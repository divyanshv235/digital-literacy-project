# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s
- Use a professional profile picture  
- Think before posting content  
- Maintain privacy settings  
- Share useful and positive content  
- Communicate respectfully  

## ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal information publicly  
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content  
- Do not engage in online arguments  
- Avoid spreading misinformation  
- Do not use abusive language
