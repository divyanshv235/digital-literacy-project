
# Cybercrime Case Study – UPI Payment Fraud

UPI payment fraud is one of the most common cybercrimes affecting students and individuals in India. In this type of fraud, scammers trick users into transferring money through fake payment requests or phishing links.

A typical scenario involves a fraudster posing as a buyer or seller on online platforms. The scammer sends a payment request instead of sending money and convinces the victim to approve it by claiming it is required to receive funds. In other cases, victims receive fake links that mimic banking or payment apps, where they unknowingly enter their sensitive details such as UPI PIN or OTP.

Students and inexperienced users are often targeted because they may not be fully aware of such tactics. The consequences include financial loss, compromised personal information, and emotional stress.

This type of fraud highlights the importance of understanding how digital payment systems work and being cautious while dealing with unknown requests online.
