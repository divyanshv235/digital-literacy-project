# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone  
- Do not approve unknown payment requests  
- Always verify the identity of the sender before transactions  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links  
- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Enable two-factor authentication where possible  
- Keep your apps and devices updated  

## UPI Safety Tips
- Remember: you do not need to enter a PIN to receive money  
- Double-check payment requests before approving  

## Reporting Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
